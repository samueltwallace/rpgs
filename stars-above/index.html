**Stars Above** This is a sandbox toolkit for sci-fi rpgs. Think [Maze Rats](https://www.drivethrurpg.com/product/197158/Maze-Rats), but in space. Appendix N is *Dune*, Dark Matter (Syfy), *Pandora's Star* (Hamilton), *Hyperion*, and the few episodes of Cowboy Bebop I've seen. These toolkit is system-agnostic, and requires less work with a minimal ruleset. You may adapt the rules from *Maze Rats,* use [24XX](https://jasontocci.itch.io/24xx), or one of your choice. These tables can be used with the standard *Maze Rats* tables. You could, for example, use the wilderness tables to generate planet biomes, and the city tables for the cities of a planet. # Items and Possessions Any items with limited uses or charges start with 3, or as many as make sense. ##
