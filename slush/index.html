**Slush Pile** Here is my public [slush pile](https://goblinpunch.blogspot.com/2014/07/how-to-be-creative-also-blobbins.html), organized by genre. # Heroic Fantasy - A group of powerful heroes set out from the last shining city of good to reconquer the evil, swampy lowlands for humanity. - System: 4e/Icon # Science Fantasy - The apocalypse is happening in every place, in every time. A post apocalyptic landscape with both teleportation circles and shuttle launch pads serve as strange, liminal dungeons. - System: Mashup of Cairn and hacks.
